Welcome to Builder

5 Years Experience

We have a diligent team of expert professionals who work with utmost dedication towards delivering the best services to our clients. Our team comprises some highly qualified and talented designers and electronic engineers who possess vast experience in their respective domain. Every team member works in close coordination with each other and they share innovative ideas to implement in existing methodology so that the process activities are carried out more efficiently and a better solution could be provided to our clients. In order to make after sales service an effective approach, we train our support team by conducting intensive training programmes regarding operation and maintenance of our systems

Our services includes video conference system installation, audio conferencing system installation, audio recording studio installation, audio equipment installation services, installation of auditorium systems, installation of P.A. & conference systems for software co., installation conference hall systems with selective microphone, installation of hospital P.A. & music system, installation of bank P.A. & music systems, installation of school p.a. music systems, installation of land scape & open air music systems, installation of audio recording studio and light systems, we use products and accessories of reputed manufacturers. This makes our systems facilitated with advanced and latest features and models. Our wide range of services finds usage in various applications like:Auditoriums,Religious places,BPOs,MNCs,Hotels,Software companies,Government and private offices,Hospitals,Schools,Colleges,Other industrial establishments......
